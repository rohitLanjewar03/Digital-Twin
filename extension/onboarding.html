Welcome to Email Assistant History Connector!

This extension connects your browsing history to your Email Assistant application to provide context-aware email suggestions and better responses. Here's how to get started:

1 Find Your User ID

Open your Email Assistant application and go to your profile settings. You'll find your User ID displayed there. Copy this ID as you'll need it to connect the extension.

Note: Your User ID is a unique identifier that helps the extension associate your browsing data with your Email Assistant account.

2 Connect the Extension

Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar, paste your User ID into the field, and click "Connect to Email Assistant".

Once connected, you'll see a green indicator showing that the extension is successfully linked to your account.

3 Sync Your History

Choose a time range and click "Sync Browsing History" to send relevant browsing data to your Email Assistant.

This will help the assistant provide more contextually relevant responses and suggestions for your emails.

Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is our priority. Here's how we protect your data:

  • History data is only sent when you explicitly click "Sync"
  • Sensitive sites (banking, health, adult content) are automatically filtered out
  • Your data is only used to improve email suggestions, never for advertising
  • All communication between the extension and Email Assistant is encrypted
  • You can disconnect or clear synced data at any time
